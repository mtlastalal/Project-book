Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and membership

How do I get a library card?
You can get a library card by visiting any of our branches with a valid ID and proof of address. Alternatively, you can register online through our website and your digital card will be activated immediately, with a physical card mailed to you within 5-7 business days.
How many books can I borrow at once?
The number of books you can borrow depends on your membership level. Basic members can borrow 2 books, Standard members can borrow 5 books, and Premium members can borrow up to 10 books at a time. There are no limits on e-books and audiobooks for Standard and Premium members.
What is your book return policy?
Books can be returned to any of our branches during opening hours, or through our after-hours drop boxes. The standard loan period is 3 weeks, but this can be extended to 4 or 6 weeks for Standard and Premium members respectively. You can renew books online up to 2 times if there are no holds on them.
Do you have books in languages other than English?
Yes, we have a growing collection of books in Spanish, French, Chinese, Arabic, and several other languages. Our digital collection also includes books in multiple languages. If you're looking for a specific title in another language, our librarians can help you locate it through interlibrary loan.
Can I access digital resources from home?
Absolutely! Once you have a library card, you can access our entire digital collection from anywhere with an internet connection. This includes e-books, audiobooks, research databases, and online learning resources. Simply log in to your account on our website or through our mobile app.
